 The CrimeWiki is an open source php based web project to put together all the information about the major criminals, crime events and criminal organisations from across the lobe. The code to create the wiki is also made open source on github at www.github.com by the Author Anupam Khosla. It is developed as a CMS(content management system). Here you can find dedicated pages on a particuar criminal as a post under the category of criminals. 

</br></br>
